// The agent pipeline

ONE PIPELINE.
END-TO-END.

BuildAgentOS runs a coordinated 6-agent pipeline — from finding your next customer to collecting their final payment. Every agent hands off to the next automatically.

01

LeadAgent — Autonomous Lead Generation

Runs every 6 hours. Scans 5 cities × 3 industries, finds businesses with no website or weak web presence, scores them 0–100 (0 = no site = hottest lead), finds contact emails via Hunter.io, and sends personalized outreach automatically. Hot leads flow directly into the pipeline.

Google Places APIHunter.ioAuto-outreachLead scoring
02

Customer Acquisition Agent — Qualify & Convert

Takes incoming leads and scores them 0–100 based on project size, timeline, location, budget, and crew availability. Scores above 70 trigger automatic site visit scheduling via SMS and calendar. Below 70 go into a nurture sequence. Handles all follow-up communication in your voice.

Lead scoringSMS/emailAuto-schedulingCRM update
03

Estimating & Bidding Agent — Instant Accurate Quotes

Analyzes project plans and photos using GPT-4 Vision, queries your historical project database for comparable jobs, pulls real-time material prices, calculates labor hours using ML models, applies your overhead and margin, and generates a professional proposal — all in seconds, 85%+ accuracy within 10% of actual cost.

Vision AIHistorical DBMaterial pricingAuto-proposal
04

Scheduling & Dispatch Agent — Crew Optimization

Solves the daily constraint optimization problem: assign the right crew (skills, certifications) to the right job, minimize total drive time, check weather for outdoor work, respect customer time windows, and handle callouts by auto-reassigning without you making a single call. Sends crew notifications automatically.

Route optimizationGoogle MapsWeather checkAuto-dispatch
05

Project Execution Agent — Site Intelligence

Monitors active jobs in real time. Analyzes progress photos from crew mobile apps, tracks milestones, detects delays before they cascade, coordinates subcontractors and material deliveries, flags change orders, and keeps clients automatically updated. Reduces human check-in calls by 80%.

Vision analysisProgress trackingChange ordersClient comms
06

Compliance & Docs Agent — Zero Violations

Tracks all permits, certifications, insurance certificates, and inspection deadlines across every active job. Auto-renews permits before expiry, generates OSHA safety checklists per job type, manages lien waivers and closeout documents, and keeps a full audit trail for SOC 2 compliance.

Permit managementOSHA checklistsAuto-renewalAudit trail
07

Billing & Collections Agent — Get Paid Faster

Generates milestone-based or T&M invoices automatically when job stages complete, processes payments via Stripe and ACH, sends intelligent payment reminders that don't damage client relationships, syncs bidirectionally with QuickBooks, and reduces your DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) by 20%+.

Auto-invoicingStripe / ACHQuickBooks syncAR aging
